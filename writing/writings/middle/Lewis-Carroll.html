The 'Lewis Carroll Poem' Poem

One day in school we were assigned To write our own descriptive poem. I looked into my empty mind I found a piece of foam. The theme for this week's poem seems Most definitely unfair.  I'll Never in my wildest dreams write quite like Lewis Carroll. I think that nonsense writing Is really quite a bore.  It Just isn't that exciting. So I think I'll just ignore it. ...And then Ms. Tran described a bit Just how the poem should writ; We had to be appropriate Which meant no saying "$@!*#." Ms. Tran told us to do something Which I remember dimly... What was it?  I'm not remembering... Oh yeah... include a simile. Similes use the word "Like," ...Now I'll have to think of one. But I can't seem to fit one in...  PSYCH! This poem is, like, done.
