A Critic’s Notations on his Book

Christian Bök’s book is shit. To start it is a task, to scan it is a strain. Its narration is as thin as hot air, as basic as ABC. In short, it stinks. Is it satiric? Sarcastic? No, its irritation is intrinsic, not ironic. Bök's book is a bionic horror, a robotic abstraction- its constant constraints constrict its chaotic consonants into crass contortions. As-is, Bök's book isn’t art. Bök's book is to art as an ant is to a rhino, as a cartoon is to a cantata, as a cat’s hiss is to Sinatra’s croons (it's a stark contrast.) Bök’s book is a scab on a cock, a crab on a hot chick’s boob, a bacon stain on a koran (or torah). Bök’s book isn’t hot shit, it’s hot snot: as I scan it, I contract bronchitis. Ick! It has no citations, it ranks on no charts, it's not canon. A kibosh on it! This book isn't a book. It’s a non-book, an anti-book. I snort at it. That as it is, Bök can't not scratch his ink nib- it's an inborn kink, a sick itch to scratch, a narcotic habit as chronic as crack, tobacco or cannabis. Not that it’s a knack- oh no. Bök’s no artist. Bök's no Tarantino, or Titian, or Bob Ross. Bök's no Bach. Bök is a hack, a con artist, a batshit kook, an anarchist crank, a Toronto hick. Bök is as skittish as an arthritic cockatoo, as brackish as a cockroach abortion, as hot as a bonobo in a bikini (AKA, not). To train Bök to concoct a book that’s not trash is to train a baboon to cook borscht. Hint, hint: Bök’s book is on track to tank. I insist it is, so bank on it. Bök’s book is as soon to sink as that boat “Titanic”, or a stock in bitcoin. Bök’s short stint in books is soon to hit a rock! Ciao, Christian! Bonsoir! Ta-ta! Thanks a ton (not!) Oh. Oh no. Oh no, no, no. NOOOOO!!! Bök’s book- it’s a hit?! It can’t- it is. Bök’s book stocks book racks in chains across nations, across China, Costa Rica, Iran, Haiti...in arabic nations (Iran, Bahrain), on to Antarctica. Cancun to Cairo, it's a hit. Bök’s stock soars. This book has kickbacks: It's a boost to his cash stash, a boon to his bankbook, a coin in his coin sack. Bök is rich. Hot to trot, Bök hobnobs hotshot critics, or chitchats to bookish brainiacs at “Christian Bök Book-a-Thons”. Ooh, it irks! Bök snacks on raisin bonbons, or has a nosh on choco-croissants in chic bistros, as snobbish hosts toast chianti to honor his book. That kiss-ass! Bök is a rockstar, an icon. Britain anoints Bök (not a Brit) “Sir Bök” in a historic coronation! Bök is an aristocrat? Oh, I abhor this- I abhor it so! I knock back a ninth scotch shot as I think. Bök a traitor to his nation, has no honor. Bök's book is a rash on this nation’s ass, a tarnish on this Christian bastion. Oh, this book is an initiation into anarchic antics too- it stirs racist associations; it starts riots. "Bök-ian" cohorts ransack train stations, accost hobos in rain-torn shacks, kick tots into trashcans. To cast this anachronistic caca in ink is an arrant sin- I can’t sanction it, not a bit. So, I chant it: Abstain! Toss Bök's Book in a bin! Roast it on a stick! Knit it into a satin boa! Crank it into thin carbon bits! Stash it in a rabbit’s ass! Bark at it! Bonk a banana on it! Or a carrot. Kick it into orbit! Boo it! Cast it into Tarnation! Shoot at it: rat-a-tat-tat! Cart it into a hot cocoa bath (so as to stain it)! Boot it back to Toronto! Boot Bök (a Torontonian) back to Toronto too! Christ, I thirst to ban Bök’s book. In a Casa Bonita, I snack on ricotta-nacho tacos. I knock back a ninth scotch shot as I think. Soon, I concoct an acrostic:
Christian: His Rot Is Soon To Inhabit A Nation Bank On Kabobs
Bank on kabobs? Ok, so this acrostic isn’t cast in iron. That as it is, I think Christian Bök is satan, an anti-Christ, or both. I scan Korans, torahs, Corinthians. Satan… Shaitan... Tchort... As it chars, I sit across a brook in a rain-torn shack. Hot soot soars into orbit; it coats a birch branch. Bök’s cabin contorts into ash. Christian Bök is toast. I sob: boo, hoo.

A notion took root, I think, at that cabin. A transition. As I think back on that occasion, I ask: is assassination OK? No. It is not OK. Assassination is a boorish, narcissistic act- not an act to boast on. I ain’t no saint; I sank to act on a barbaric instinct. I shat on a sacrosanct contract, I took a torch to a tacit oath. I shirk to think it! So. No snark, no schtick: assassination is a no-no, OK? Arson too. Ok. Thanks. That's it.
